# RepLift Brand Guide

## Positioning

RepLift is the nutrition tracker for people who take both their training **and their data** seriously.
Differentiator: engineering honesty — logs never disappear, search actually understands you, numbers are deterministic.

**Tagline:** *Eat with intent. Lift your limits.*

## Voice & tone

| Trait | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Direct** | "Log food", "3 changes waiting to sync" | "Leverage our synergistic logging solution" |
| **Encouraging, never preachy** | "Consistency beats perfection — try logging just breakfast daily." | Guilt-tripping ("You failed your goal") |
| **Honest about limits** | "Estimates to start from, not medical advice." | Health claims, diagnosis language, hype |
| **Calm under failure** | "You're offline — changes are saved locally and will sync when you reconnect." | Alarmist errors, blame ("You lost connection!") |
| **Playfully confident (marketing only)** | "Free is genuinely useful. Pro is for the obsessed." | Jokes inside error states or health data surfaces |

Rules: sentence case everywhere (no Title Case buttons) · numbers always tabular · never bury a failure ("saved locally" beats a silent spinner) · exercise credit is framed positive (`+320 kcal`), overshoot framed factual, not moralizing ("312 kcal over", never "you cheated").

## Visual identity

- **Mark:** figure + dumbbell + leaf (black/white). On dark tiles or `--primary` gradient tiles. Clear space = ¼ mark width.
- **Wordmark:** `Rep` in foreground color + `Lift` in primary orange, Plus Jakarta Sans 700–800.
- **Palette:** Energy Orange `#F97316` (action/brand) · Success Green `#22C55E` (goals met) · slate neutrals · macro colors fixed: protein blue / carbs amber / fat violet / water sky.
- **Type:** Plus Jakarta Sans (300–800). Headings tracking-tight; metrics `tabular-nums`.
- **Imagery/motifs:** ring gauges, macro bars, sync states — the product IS the imagery. No stock gym photos.
- **Motion:** 150–300 ms, ease-out, purposeful (entrance, state change). Never decorative loops.

## Naming conventions

Diary (not "food log page") · Log (verb, primary action) · Serving (not "portion") · Goal (not "plan") · Sync (user-visible word for the outbox machinery) · "This device / Other device" in conflict UI.
